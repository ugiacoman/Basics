QUESTION 3:
What was printed by each of the commands? Why were there different
results for each command (i.e. indicate why each result was produced)? 

ANSWER:
77
14

In the first command, two strings were added, hence 77. In the second, two
integers were added, hence 14. The computer recognized "7" as a string not 
an integer and therefore just placed them next to each other.
